For those just getting into comics the subject may seem a bit overwhelming...

...and it is. Comics is a huge medium which has been around for longer than you might think and although it can come across as trashy or even trivial there's no denying it's popularity.

I've been reading comics my self for about 26 years and have found in that time that the range of different styles and subjects in comics is huge, much bigger than I initially would have thought (although at 7 years I wasn't really aware of ....well anything!).

Most people start off being aware of Superheroes or maybe even The Beano or Dandy comics so if neither of those peak their interest then that's about as far as it goes. But really comics is a much broader medium than you might think ranging from sci-fi to sports, romance, drama, horror, mystery, auto-biography, historical, art-house... you name it. Now, if you're like me, then you won't see any point in wasting time going through piles of bad comics, or even mediocre ones because honestly who's got time for that? Well, kids do, and I did as kid but I was an idiot! So if you're not an idiot then you've come to the right place. Check out my reviews here and get reading some of the best comics out there...
